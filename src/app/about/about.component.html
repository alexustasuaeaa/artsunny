A person needs meaning, a goal to strive for. It’s important to understand why you woke up today and why you have to crawl out from under a warm blanket and leave the comfort of your bed.

Creativity, for me, is an opportunity to interact with something or someone. It is a process of discovery, a revelation in the moment of creation that comes after a long period of contemplation.

From what I’ve managed to understand by thirty: when everything around seems indecent or incomprehensible, you can always stop and take a pause. Read Hemingway’s “A Clean, Well-Lighted Place” or listen to Curtis Stigers – “Let’s Go Out Tonight”. Believe that getting lost in Van Gogh’s “The Cottages” is far better than getting lost in the chaos of modern reality.
